Car fails to start

Symptoms

  • Engine does not crank when you turn the key
  • Clicking sound but engine won't start
  • No sound at all when starting
  • Dashboard lights dim or off
  • Engine cranks but does not start
  • Car start sometimes, sometimes not

Faulty parts (Causes)

  • Dead or weak battery
  • Loose or corroded battery terminals
  • Faulty starter motor
  • Bad ignition switch
  • Fuel problem (empty tank, clogged fuel filter, bad fuel pump)
  • Faulty spark plugs
  • Blown fuse or relay
  • Immobilizer or key issue

Easy hacks or Temporary tricks

  • Try jump-starting the car
  • Tighten loose batter terminals
  • Clean battery terminals (if accessible)
  • Turn key to ON position for 5 to 10 seconds, then start
  • Try a spare key (immobilizer issue)
  • if manual car: push start(temporary and only if safe)

Note: These are short term fixes, not permanent.

Time required to fix

  • Jump start/ terminal fix: 10-15 minutes
  • Battery replacement: 15-20 minutes
  • Starter or fuel system repair: 1-3 hours

Cost level

  • Terminal cleaning / fuse: Low
  • Battery replacement: Medium
  • Starter motor / fuel pump: High

Proper Solution (Permanent Fix)

  • Replace weak or dead battery
  • Fix or replace starter motor
  • Repair fuel system (pump,filter)
  • Replace spark plugs if worn
  • Check and replace faulty fuses or relays
  • Diagnose immobilizer or ignition switch

When to Visit a Mechanic

  • Car does not start even after jump-start
  • Repeated clicking sound
  • Strong fuel smell but no start
  • Starter motor makes grinding noise
  • Car stalls and refuses to start
