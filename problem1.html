Car Tyre Puncture

Symptoms

  • Tyre looks visibly flat or low on air
  • Car pulls to one side while driving
  • Steering feels heavy or unstable
  • Thumping or flapping sound while driving
  • Tyre pressure warning lights turns on (in new cars)

Faulty parts (causes)

  • Tyre itself (nail,screw,glass,sharp object)
  • Valve stem leaking air
  • Rim damage(less commom)

Easy hacks / Temporary tricks

  • Use the spare tyre if available
  • Inflate the tyre using a portable air pump to reach apuncture shop
  • If puncture is very small, drive slowly and avoid sharp turns
  • Do not overload the car
  • Keep speed low and drive only short distances

Note: These are only temporary. Driving long with a punctured tyre can damage the rim.

Time required to fix

  • Short time (usually done within minutes)

Cost level

  • Cheap (Simple puncture repair is one of the lowest cost car fixes

Proper Solutions

  1. Remove the tyre
  2. Locate the puncture
  3. Repair using patch or plug method (from inside is better)
  4. Check tyre balance if needed
  5. Replace tyre if puncture is on sidewall (cannot be safely repaired)

When to visit a mechanic immediately

  • Tyre goes flat again and again
  • Puncture is on sidewall
  • Tyre has multiple punctures
  • Tyre is old and worn out
  • You feel vibration even after repair
