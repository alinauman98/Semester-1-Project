Brake Fails

Symptoms

  • Brake pedal goes very soft to the floor
  • Car does not stop properly
  • Grinding or squealing noise when braking
  • Brake warning lights turns ON
  • Car pulls to one side when braking
  • Burning smell from wheels

Faulty Parts (Causes)

  • Low or leaking brake fluid
  • Worn brake pads or shoes
  • Air in brake lines
  • Brake master cylinder failure
  • Faulty brake booster
  • Overheated brakes (brake fade)
  • Damaged brake lines

Easy hacks/ Temporary tricks

  • Pump the brake pedal repeatedly
  • Use engine braking (downshift gears)
  • Use handbreak slowly (never pull suddenly)
  • Switch on hazard lights
  • Move car to the side safely
  • Note: Do not drive normally with break issues.

Time Required to Fix

  • Brake fluid top-up and bleeding: 30-60 minutes
  • Pad or shoe replacement: 1-2 hours
  • Master cylinder/ brake line repair: 2-4 hours

Cost Level

  • Brake fluid or bleeding: Low
  • Pad replacement: Medium
  • Master cylinder/ brake line repair: High

Proper Solution

  • Fix brake fluid leaks
  • Replace worn brake/
