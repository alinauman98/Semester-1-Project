Engine Trouble Sounds (Unusual Noises)

Symptoms

  • Knocking or tapping sound from engine
  • Hissing sound
  • Grinding or metal-to-metal noise
  • Squealing noise when starting or accelerating
  • Rattling sound, especially on rough roads
  • Engine sounds louder than normal

Faulty Parts (Causes)

  • Low engine oil or poor quality oil
  • Worn engine bearings
  • Loose belts (serpentine belt)
  • Faulty timing belt or chain
  • Exhaust leak
  • Loose heat shield or engine cover
  • Worn spark plugs
  • Engine misfire

Easy hacks/ Temporary tricks

  • Check and top up engine oil
  • Turn off AC to reduce engine load
  • Drive at low RPM and avoid hard acceleration
  • Tighten any visible loose covers

Time Required to Fix

  • Oil top-up: 15-30 minutes
  • Belt tightening/replacement: 30-60 minutes
  • Timing or internal engine work: 3-6 hours

Cost level

  • Oil top-up or loose cover: Low
  • Belt replacement or spark plugs: Medium
  • Engine internal repair: High

Proper Solution

  • Maintain correct engine oil level and quality
  • Replace worn belts or timing components
  • Fix exhaust leaks
  • Replace damaged spark plugs
  • Secure or replace loose heat shields

When to Visit a Mechanic

  • Loud knocking or metal grinding sound
  • Noise increases with acceleration
  • Engine warning light turns on
  • Loss of power with noise
  • Burning smell along with sound
