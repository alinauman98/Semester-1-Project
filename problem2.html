Car lights not working

Symptoms

  • Headlights, brake lights, or indicators don't turn on
  • Lights flicker or work sometimes, sometimes don't
  • Dashboard shows light warning symbols

Faulty Parts (Causes)

  • Blown bulb
  • Blown fuse
  • Looose or corroded wiring
  • Faulty light switch
  • Weak battery (if multiple lights fail).

Easy hacks

  • Gently tap the light housing (works if bulb filament is loose)
  • Switch lights off and on again
  • Check and replace fuse (easy and quick)
  • Clean bulb holder if dirty
  • Use daytime driving only until fixed

Time required to fix

Short time (bulb or fuse replacement is quick)

Cost level

Cheap to moderate (Bulbs are cheap, wiring or switch repair can raise cost

Proper Solution

  • Identify which light is not working
  • Replace the bulb if blown
  • Check and replace fuse if needed
  • Inspect wiring for damage rust
  • Repair or replace light switch if faulty.

When to visit mechanic?

  • Multiple lights stop working at once
  • Lights keep blowing after replacement
  • Headlights stop working at night
  • Burning smell or exposed wires
  • Lights fail along with other electrical issues.
